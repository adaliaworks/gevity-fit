Your first 90 days,
already charted.

Most apps start empty. Gevity starts full — with a realistic picture of what your next three months will look like if you just keep going.

1

Tell us how you train

A few quick questions — how many days a week, what kind of workouts, any cardio goals. This takes about three minutes.

2

We fill in the picture

Gevity pre-loads 90 days of your personalised history — your workouts, rest days, even a vacation week. Imperfect by design, because life happens.

3

You make it real, one day at a time

Every workout you log replaces a fictional one. Our fictional history becomes yours for real. Come back in 90 days and see the difference.

The science

You don't have to overhaul your life.
The research agrees.

The longevity science is almost boringly simple. A few brisk walks or easy bike rides a week, a little strength work, and — the hard part — consistency. You don't need two-hour workouts. In fact, the benefit of strength training to your lifespan maxes out at under an hour a week.

~150 min
moderate movement a week

Three or four brisk walks or easy rides gets most people there. No gym required.

30–60 min
strength work a week

Where the longevity benefit peaks. More than that doesn't help you live longer.

For years
the part that actually matters

Consistency beats intensity. That's the exact part Gevity is built to help with.
